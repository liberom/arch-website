How It Works

The 4-Step Discovery Framework

We don't guess. We measure. Every engagement starts with a structured audit that identifies exactly where AI can deliver ROI — ranked, priced, and ready to build.

Step 1

Capture Reality

We interview the people doing the work — not just managers. We document every handoff, tool, and bottleneck. What actually happens, not what the SOP says.

Step 2

Quantify Volume

How often does this run? How many people touch it? We convert every process into hard numbers — frequency, headcount, hours per week.

Step 3

Price the Pain

Minutes × frequency × loaded labor rate = real cost. We show you exactly what each inefficiency is costing you in dollars per month.

Step 4

Prioritize by ROI

Conservative, expected, and optimistic scenarios. Ranked. You pick what to build first based on impact, complexity, and budget.

FAQ

Got questions?

What's the difference between the Assessment and a full build?
The Assessment is a diagnostic — we audit your workflows, identify AI opportunities, and deliver an ROI-prioritized report. The Implementation is the build — we actually deploy the workflows on your infrastructure. Many clients start with the Assessment and expand from there.
Do I need my own server or VPS?
Yes. Everything we build runs on your infrastructure via Docker Compose. We recommend a $10-30/month VPS (DigitalOcean, Hetzner, or similar). We handle the setup — you just need the account.
How long does a typical project take?
An Assessment takes 2-3 weeks. A single workflow runs 3-6 weeks. Multi-workflow packages span 6-12 weeks. We provide a timeline during the proposal phase so you know exactly what to expect.
What happens after the project is done?
You own everything — the code, the workflows, the documentation. We provide 30 days of free post-launch support. After that, you can continue independently or sign up for an Ongoing AI Partnership retainer.
Who is this NOT for?
If you're looking for a quick chatbot widget or a one-click SaaS, this isn't it. Arch builds serious infrastructure — it requires a VPS and a willingness to own your automation. If you want done-for-you without the ownership, check out Vynix Cortex.
